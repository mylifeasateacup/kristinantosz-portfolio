Kristin Antosz

UX Designer & Front-end Developer

Hi there, I'm Kristin!

My goal is to make a user experience so intuitive that users, frankly, don't notice it. Yet, it takes more than just one UX specialist to transform an experience into one that's enjoyable for users, and effective for a company. I believe in the importance of cross-functional teams (designing in a bubble helps no one.) I believe in the voice of the customer when designing any digital experience. I believe in iterating, in testing and in data. And I believe in telling a story - empathy and feeling play just as critical a role as conversions.

Oh, and I used to be a high school English teacher.
