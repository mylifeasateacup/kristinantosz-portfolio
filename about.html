About Kristin

I believe in the importance of cross-functional teams (designing in a bubble helps no one.) I believe in the voice of the customer when designing any digital experience. I believe in iterating, in testing and in data; while there are good principles to follow, every audience comes with unique needs. And I believe in telling a story - empathy and feeling play just as critical a role as conversions.

I currently serve as a UX consultant, web designer, and developer at MSA - The Safety Company and manage over 25 web properties worldwide. For a company that prides themselves on customer-centric service at every level, I bring a user-focused UX strategy to our web presence, from our e-commerce experience to individual marketing landing pages. As part of the marketing operations team, I provide digital solutions so that marketing can execute their strategy in a way that is technologically savvy, accessible, user-centric, and keeps performance in mind. I serve as a resource for our global marketing teams on digital experience, design, and user-experience best practices and collaborate with global marketing teams to design responsive landing pages that are elegant, efficient, and convert.

Championing user research is near and dear to me; I have a passion for turning customer feedback into solutions that improve the use of our website and e-commerce experience for our users. I've taken frustrating checkout experiences with dead ends and unclear jargon and turned them into clear steps in our checkout process.

My background in marketing and teaching high school literature may be non-traditional, but it allows me to draw from a pretty big arsenal of communication skills, including relaying design decisions, explaining the why behind processes, involving the many voices and perspectives on a team, and the importance of clear communication.
